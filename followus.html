Trex Game Made By Fiona

Game is also known as Google Chrome Dinosaur Game. When browsing offline in Chrome, a message will appear stating that they are not connected to the internet. Above this message is an image of a dinosaur, the "Lonely T-Rex". One can activate the game called "T-Rex Runner" by pressing space, the up arrow or the down arrow, and the player will be able to control the Lonely T-Rex with these buttons.
